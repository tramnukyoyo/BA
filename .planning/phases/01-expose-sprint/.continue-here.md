## üß† Mental Context / Approach

### Why This Methodology?

**Benchmarking (Vergleichende Analyse):**
- Systematically compares 3 regions across 4 dimensions
- Objective, data-driven (not opinion-based)
- Shows what works where and why
- Identifies best practices and region-specific barriers

**Cost-Benefit Analysis (CBA):**
- Quantifies economic viability
- Calculates NPV, ROI, break-even points
- Shows which strategies have strongest economics
- Makes recommendations measurable

**Why together?** Because infrastructure investment decisions need BOTH objective comparison AND economic justification. Your thesis will show WHAT strategies exist (benchmarking) and WHETHER they make economic sense (CBA).

### The Thesis Flow

```
Einleitung (Why this matters)
    ‚Üì
Theoretische Grundlagen (What is hydrogen? Supply chain concepts? Barriers?)
    ‚Üì
Methodik (HOW we will analyze: Benchmarking framework + CBA model)
    ‚Üì

    ‚Üì
Diskussion (WHAT IT MEANS: Interpret findings, answer RQs, limitations)
    ‚Üì
Fazit (SO WHAT: Recommendations and implications)
```
