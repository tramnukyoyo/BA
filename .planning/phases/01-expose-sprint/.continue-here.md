## Key Methodological Checkpoint

### Why Benchmarking + CBA?

**Benchmarking (Vergleichende Analyse):**
- Compares 3 regions (EU, Asia-Pacific, Australia) systematically
- Evaluates 4 dimensions: regulatory, technical, economic, logistical
- Identifies best practices and region-specific barriers
- Objective, data-driven comparison

**Cost-Benefit Analysis (CBA):**
- Quantifies economic viability of different infrastructure models
- Calculates NPV (Net Present Value) for scenarios
- Makes findings measurable and decision-ready
- Answers "which strategy has best ROI?"

**Together:** Provides evidence-based recommendations, not opinion-based
